Flexible & affordable PRICING

The CBC App pricing is subscription based and prices depend on the nature of the subscription.

The two main types of subscriptions are Individual Teacher subscriptions and School subscriptions.

Under the School subscriptions, we have special pricing for public schools and low-cost private schools.

For Schools

Subscription price

–Based on the number of learners in the CBC classes

–Ksh.450 per-learner per-year

–One-time set-up and training fees charged

Register now

For Individual Teachers

Subscription price

–Based on the number of classes a teacher selects

–Ksh.250 per-month for the first two classes

–Ksh.25 for each additional class afterwards

Special consideration for public schools and low-cost private schools

We have a special pricing consideration for public schools and low-cost private schools. Through this special consideration, we hope to create an opportunity for these schools to get access to tools such as the CBC App, which are typically not accessible to them. We thank the schools using the CBC App for making this initiative possible and in helping us uplift our public and low-cost private schools.
