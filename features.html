FEATURES of the CBC App

There are two versions of the CBC App:

–A School Version

–An Individual Teacher Version

Compare Prices

SIMPLE

Fast and efficient assessment

In order to use rubrics for assessment under CBC, a teacher typically has to spend a lot of time drawing activity sheets on paper and then writing down all activities and all student names.

The CBC App simplifies this entire process by creating the same activity sheet for you, automatically prefilling your pupils on the sheet and presenting to you rubric templates for every strand and sub-strand.

All you have to do is click to assign your pupils to the various performance levels. This saves a lot of time and makes it possible to perform assessments many times without it being a burden.

CUSTOM

Customise KICD rubrics or create your own rubrics

KICD provides assessment rubrics that you can use to assess each strand and sub-strand in the curriculum.

The CBC App presents the KICD rubrics in a way that makes it very easy for you to use them as they are or modify. If you prefer to create your own rubrics from scratch, the CBC App allows you to do this and the rubrics you create can be reused several times over in the future.

DYNAMIC

Produce formative assessment reports like never before

Developing formative assessment reports, and especially out of assessment rubrics, has always been a challenge for teachers and schools. The CBC App now makes it possible to produce reports about students, subjects, strands and sub-strands in great detail. The reports are highly customisable through the report filters that allow you to select what you want to see in the report.

SHARE

Print and securely share reports

With the CBC App you can easily print dynamic reports and get downloadable versions in .pdf format.

You can also share these dynamic reports via SMS or WhatsApp using a secure shareable link that requires a pin for recipients to view its contents.

MANAGE

Efficiently manage student records

The CBC App offers is an easy way to keep track of your student records and to group them into different grades. Add, remove or edit your student details.

Once your student records are added, they will automatically be loaded into assessment sheets and grade report sheets.

FLEXIBLE

Configure school options

The CBC App gives the flexibility to managers and administrators to flexibly configure school details in ways that suit the school's specific situation.

These flexible configuration options make the data recording more accurate and reports more accurate.

MANAGEMENT

Manage classes and students centrally

The CBC App allows you to centrally manage the creation of classes, streams and the allocation of students. This gives the school the right to properly administer the school's record keeping.

ADMINISTRATION

Manage teachers centrally

Teacher management is one of the CBC App's most important features since it is the teachers that create the assessment records.

This feature makes it possible for school managers and administrators to allocate learning areas and classes to teachers, and to have the flexibility to manage teacher access to student records.

DETAILED REPORTS

Detailed report dashboards

The School Version of the CBC App gives school managers and administrators an overall view of assessment activities across the entire school as well as fine details across all the aspects of reporting.

From student, to learning area, to individual teacher reports, the CBC App can give schools very insightful reports that can lead to better decisions and more informed actions that will lead to transformation in the way education is delivered.
